Deno 2 vs Bun Benchmarks

Disclaimer:
These benchmarks were made out of curiosity and most likely won't reflect
real world scenarios that your application is going to face.

You may notice we're focusing only on requests per second which is definitely not the only relevant metric in a benchmark. Latency, RAM & CPU usage, operational system and code architecture are other variables extremely important. That's exactly why you should not use the results below as the cornerstone to chose either Bun nor Deno.

You also may want to run your own fine tuned benchmarks, focused on your use case.
The ones presented below are just for fun.

  • Executed at: 21 September 2024
  • Deno version: 2.0.3
